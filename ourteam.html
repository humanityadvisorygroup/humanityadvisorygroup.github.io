Meet Our Team

Experts in strategy. Advocates for people. Partners in transformation.

Ryan J. Peterson

Chief Executive Officer

Ryan is a healthcare consultant and CEO with over 10 years of experience in the pharmaceutical industry, specializing in strategy, regulatory affairs, and patient-centered innovation. He holds a Master of Science in Health Law and Strategy from New York University and a bachelor's degree in History and Political Science from Washington State University. With a deep understanding of complex healthcare systems, he advises clients on developing tailored, policy-informed solutions that drive meaningful impact and sustainable growth.

Matthew J. Johnson DO, MS, MBA

Chief Medical Officer & Chief Strategy Officer

Dr. Johnson is a Yale-trained, board-certified psychiatrist, Marine Corps veteran, and law student, and is dedicated to advancing transformative changes in healthcare and veteran advocacy. He is driven by a commitment to service, strategic leadership, and mentorship.

He completed his psychiatry residency at Yale University, where he served as Chief Resident and was awarded the teacher and mentor of the year award, during which he simultaneously earned his MBA from the University of Massachusetts Amherst’s Isenberg School of Management. He holds a medical degree (D.O.) from Rowan University, master’s degree (M.S.) in health law and strategy from New York University School of Law, and bachelor’s degrees (B.S.) in Health Sciences and Biopsychology from Philadelphia University (now Thomas Jefferson University). He is currently a law student (JD) at Fordham University School of Law.

Dr. Johnson owns and operates Encompass Psychiatry LLC; a consulting practice focused on independent medical consultations for veterans seeking VA benefits. His military background, including deployments to Afghanistan and multiple personal commendations, continues to shape his dedication to advocacy and mentorship. He is also a co-founder and both the Chief Medical Officer and Chief Strategy Officer for Humanity Advisory Group, a healthcare strategy and consulting firm where he can drive his expertise at the crossroads of medicine, business, and law. Additionally, he is the current behavioral health Medical Director for NJ Medicaid through Elevance Health and is the former Medical Director of RWJ University Hospital systems eating disorders programming. He holds a professorship with Rutgers RWJ Medical School.

Dr. Johnson has published and presented research on veteran health, PTSD, addiction, cancer, cardiac disease, integrative medicine, eating disorders, and general psychiatric conditions. His most recent work includes a textbook chapter on integrative medicine in psychiatry and on refeeding-induced recurrence of tardive dyskinesia. He has a paper pending peer review on treatment of tianeptine and kratom misuse and is writing a paper on delayed onset hepatic steatosis amid static NG nutrition.

Dr. Johnson strives for excellence and accountability in his endeavors and aims to continue growing his fund of knowledge and experience while mentoring and advising others for the same.
